Frequently asked questions

Answers to our most frequently asked questions are just one click away.

  • Nike offers several delivery options depending on your location. Standard shipping usually takes a few business days, while faster methods like Express delivery are available at checkout. Shipping fees and times are shown before you place your order.

  • When you shop Nike online, either in the Nike App or on Nike.com, you can return items that are unworn and unwashed and still have their product tags attached within 30 days of purchase (some exceptions apply). That includes custom Nike By You sneakers.

  • After placing an order, you’ll receive a confirmation email with tracking details once your package ships. You can also track your order anytime in the Nike App or on Nike.com by signing in to your account and checking your order history.

  • Nike provides detailed size guides for every product and in the Nike App. You can compare your measurements with the guide to choose the best fit. Some items also feature «Find Your Fit» tools for extra accuracy.
